Experiences

Adapakala Manoj

Disease: Hypertension

Symptoms : I experienced frequent headaches, dizziness, and occasional shortness of breath. My blood pressure readings were consistently above 140/90 mmHg.

Precautionary Measures: I started exercising regularly (30 minutes of walking daily), reduced salt intake, and practiced relaxation techniques like deep breathing and meditation.

Doctor Consulted:I visited a cardiologist who conducted an ECG and prescribed lifestyle changes.

Diet: I followed a DASH (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) diet, which includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and low-fat dairy products.

Medication:- The doctor prescribed amlodipine (5 mg daily) to manage my blood pressure. I also take an occasional aspirin (75 mg) for heart health.

Temburu Akhil

Asthma

Symptoms :I had frequent wheezing, coughing, shortness of breath, and tightness in my chest, especially after physical activity or exposure to cold air.

Precautionary Measures: I started using an air purifier at home, avoided allergens like dust, and practiced breathing exercises to improve lung capacity.

Doctor Consulted:I consulted a pulmonologist who conducted spirometry tests and adjusted my treatment plan.

Diet: I maintained a healthy diet that included anti-inflammatory foods like omega-3-rich fish, green vegetables, and fresh fruits while avoiding dairy and processed foods that can exacerbate asthma symptoms.

Medication:- The doctor prescribed a combination of an inhaler (salbutamol for immediate relief) and a corticosteroid (fluticasone) for long-term control. I also take montelukast (10 mg) daily.

Pritish Kumar Sahoo

Disease: Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD)

Symptoms : I experienced chronic heartburn, regurgitation, and chest pain, especially after meals or when lying down.

Precautionary Measures:I raised the head of my bed, avoided lying down immediately after meals, and reduced intake of spicy, fatty, and acidic foods.

Doctor Consulted: I saw a gastroenterologist who performed an endoscopy to rule out more severe issues and recommended dietary changes.

Diet: My diet is low in acidic foods, with frequent small meals, avoiding large meals late at night. I focus on high-fiber foods and avoid caffeine, alcohol, and chocolate.

Medication:I was prescribed a proton pump inhibitor (omeprazole 20 mg) to reduce stomach acid production and antacids for occasional relief.
